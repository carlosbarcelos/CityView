CityView Information

The CityView system is entirely hosted on a website and runs off of simple HTML and JavaScript. The data visualization is a product of applying CSV files of spatial-temporal data to a map provided by CARTO, MapBox, or Leaflet. The purpose of the project is to demonstrate how data visualization can be used for urban development planning in the real-world as a better option than simple data on a page.
